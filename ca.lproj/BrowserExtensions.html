Install Browser Extensions

Enjoy true integration with your web browsers.

  • Automatically save and fill website logins. It will detect when you log into a site and will prompt you to save the new Login to use later.

  • Fill addresses, contact information, forums, and more. You can save all or some of this information in a 1Password Identity, and you can create as many Identities as you like.

  • Ompli fàcilment targetes de crèdit mentre compra en línia. Compri més còmodament i protegeixi la seguretat del seu número de la targeta de crèdit emmagatzemant-lo xifrat localment a la cartera de 1Password.

  • Generate incredibly strong passwords while 1Password remembers all your passwords so you don’t have to. The days of using your pet’s name or anniversary are long gone.

Assistent de 1Password

L'assistent s'executara a la barra de menú.

It will keep your browsers in sync, let you use keyboard shortcuts, lock and unlock 1Password, and automatically back up your data even when 1Password is closed.
